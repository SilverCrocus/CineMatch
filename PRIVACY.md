# Privacy Policy for Cinematch

**Last updated: December 30, 2025**

## Overview

Cinematch ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google to create your account.

### Usage Data
- Movie preferences (likes/dislikes) to provide personalized recommendations
- Session data when participating in group movie matching sessions

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app's functionality
- To enable group session features with friends
- To personalize your movie recommendations
- To improve our services

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Sign-In** for authentication
- **TMDB (The Movie Database)** for movie information and images

## Data Retention

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy
